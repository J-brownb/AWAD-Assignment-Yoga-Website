Yoga Categories

Within our database, we have several different categories of yoga to ensure you get the full benefit. From core yoga poses to chest opening yoga poses, having a well rounded routine will maximise the effectiveness of yoga on your overall mental and physical health.


Core

Engage your abdominal muscles with core yoga poses that build a strong and stable center like Boat Pose, Dolphin Pose and Side Plank Pose.


Seated

Stay supple in your yoga practice with twisting asanas. Tone the belly, massage your internal organs, and relieve lower back pain.


Strengthening

Work and tone your entire body with strengthening yoga poses like Chair Pose, Warrior I Pose and Extended Side Angle Pose.


Chest Opening

Open your heart and shoulders and relax with chest opening yoga poses which include poses like Camel Pose, Fish Pose and Wild Thing


Backbends

Discover the powerful effects of yoga backbends to help relax your body, relieve stress and strengthen your muscles.


Forward Bend

Learn how to work stiff muscles safely, promote lower-body flexibility, and find correct alignment with forward bend yoga poses.
